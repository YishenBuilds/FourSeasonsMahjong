Four Seasons Mahjong Privacy Policy

Updated: June 1, 2026

App: Four Seasons Mahjong

No User Data Collected

Four Seasons Mahjong does not collect, upload, or share personally identifiable information. The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or gameplay behavior.

On-Device Storage

The app stores a small amount of preferences and progress locally on the device, such as language, sound, haptics, theme selection, completed board count, and recent board de-duplication data. This data remains on the user's device and is not uploaded to developer servers.

No Ads, Tracking, or Analytics

The app does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs. It does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

Purchases

Theme unlocks use Apple StoreKit. Payment, transaction history, and refunds are handled by Apple. The developer does not receive full payment information.

Network

Core gameplay does not require an account, login, or cloud sync. Runtime assets are bundled locally with the app and are not downloaded from developer servers.

四季麻将隐私政策

更新日期：2026 年 6 月 1 日

适用应用：四季麻将（Four Seasons Mahjong）

我们不收集用户数据

四季麻将不收集、上传或共享可识别个人身份的信息。应用不会收集姓名、邮箱、电话号码、位置、联系人、照片、设备广告标识符或游戏行为数据。

本机保存

应用会在设备本地保存少量偏好与进度，例如语言、声音、触感、主题选择、已完成局数和最近牌局去重信息。这些数据只保存在用户设备上，不会上传到开发者服务器。

无广告、无追踪、无分析

应用不包含广告 SDK、第三方分析 SDK 或第三方追踪 SDK，不会请求 App Tracking Transparency 追踪权限。

购买

主题解锁使用 Apple StoreKit。支付、交易记录和退款等由 Apple 处理。开发者不会接触完整支付信息。

网络

应用核心玩法无需账号、无需登录、无需云同步。运行素材随 App 本地打包，不从开发者服务器下载。
