Common Questions

Is this four-player mahjong?
No. Four Seasons Mahjong is a tile-matching game.

Does the app require internet?
Core gameplay does not require internet. Theme purchases and restore purchases use Apple StoreKit.

Does the app include ads or tracking?
No. The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Who handles purchases?
Theme unlock purchases are handled by Apple. Refunds, billing, and Family Sharing follow Apple's rules.
